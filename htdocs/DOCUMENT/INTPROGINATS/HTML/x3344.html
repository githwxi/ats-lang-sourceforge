Linear Arrays

Arrays are definable in ATS. More specifically, array views, that is, views describing arrays, can be defined in terms of at-views. 

dataview array_v
  (a:viewt@ype+, int(*size*), addr(*beg*)) =
  | {n:nat} {l:addr}
    array_v_cons (a, n+1, l) of (a @ l, array_v (a, n, l+sizeof a))
  | {l:addr} array_v_nil (a, 0, l)
